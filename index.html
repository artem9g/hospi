White Oak Hospice provides four dedicated levels of care all depends on your needs:
Routine Home Care
We monitor patients' condition from visit to visit, keeping the plan of care updated to make sure the needs of our patients and their families are met.
Respite Care
This level of care focuses on caregivers and family members. It is limited to five (5) consecutive days at a time.
Continuous Care
Continuous care provides more intense care in the patient's home environment.
Inpatient Care
The inpatient level of care is provided as needed, in a contracted facility, for patients who require a higher level of skilled care to manage complicated symptom.
White Oak Hospice is here for you 24 hours a day, 7 days week.
Qualified personnel
Our compassionate group of medical and social experts will unite with your team of care providers to provide specialized services to help meet your specific needs.
Differentiated Service
Our core team includes experienced Hospice Care Physicians, compassionate Licensed Nurses, Social Workers, Spiritual Advisors, Massage Therapies, Nutritionists and Pharmacists.
Individual approach
There is an individual plan of care for each patient. The level of care depends on specific needs and requests made by our patient and their family members.

Who pays for Hospice services:

Medicare, Medicaid and most private insurance policies have benefit that will pay for hospice services related to the hospice diagnosis.

The patient continues to be covered by Medicare, Medicaid or private insurance for treatment of any unrelated diagnosis or medical problems.

We will help you to determine the hospice benefit you have available.
